Welcome to RaceX

RaceX is a fun racing simulator where you choose your racers, set their speed rules, and watch them compete to the finish line.

How to Play

  • Go to Settings to configure race options (speed, number of racers, etc.).
  • Go to Race, pick your racers, and start the race.
  • View the Results to see who won!
